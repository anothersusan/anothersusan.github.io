My Resume

My resume is crazy long so I am going to send you to my LinkedIn page. You can decided if it's worth reading the whole thing. But here are some key summary points:

  • I'm an instructor at NSS because the user's experience IS part of the software development life cycle. Why build something no one would use? or buy!?!
  • I have been freelancing for 6 years after many different jobs (again, go here for more on that).
  • Industries I have crafted user experiences for: healthcare, law, finance, security, intelligence, music, cable, higher education... I know there's more but that's all that comes to mind right now.
  • People often ask what my favorite projects are: Putting the first complete cable TV app on an iPad was awesome. Online medical triage app was a fabulous challenge. I hate dashboards because they are over used and cliché but I designed some for intel analysts that quickly made a real, measurable difference in their work after we launched.
  • PREACH: User Experience (UX) is not the UI, but the UI is part of the UX.
